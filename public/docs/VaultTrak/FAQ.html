Frequently Asked Questions

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

General Questions

Q: What is VaultTrak?
A: VaultTrak is a secure time tracking application designed for professionals who need to track billable hours, manage projects, and generate detailed reports. It features end-to-end encryption, multi-device sync, and powerful analytics.
Q: What platforms does VaultTrak support?
A: VaultTrak is available on:
  • iOS (iPhone and iPad)
  • Android
  • macOS
  • Web browser
All platforms sync seamlessly through your account.
Q: Is there a free version?
A: Yes! VaultTrak offers a free tier that includes:
  • Unlimited time tracking
  • Up to 3 projects
  • Basic reports
  • Manual data export
Premium features require a paid subscription.

Account & Subscription

Q: How do I upgrade to a paid plan?
A: To upgrade:
  1. Go to Settings → Subscription
  2. Choose a plan (Pro or Business)
  3. Enter payment information
  4. Confirm upgrade
Your new features are available immediately.
Q: Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
A: Yes! You can cancel anytime through Settings → Subscription → Cancel. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period. Your data remains available even after cancellation, though some features will be limited.
Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied, contact support@vaulttrak.com within 30 days for a full refund.

Features & Functionality

Q: How does automatic time tracking work?
A: VaultTrak can automatically track time based on:
  • Calendar events (syncs with your calendar)
  • Application usage (desktop only)
  • Location (with your permission)
  • Manual timers you start/stop
You can enable/disable these features in Settings → Time Tracking.
Q: Can I edit time entries after they're created?
A: Yes! Simply tap on any time entry to edit:
  • Start and end times
  • Project and task
  • Notes and tags
  • Billing status
All changes are synced across your devices.
Q: How many projects can I create?
A: It depends on your plan:
  • Free: 3 projects
  • Pro: Unlimited projects
  • Business: Unlimited projects + team collaboration
Q: Can I track time for multiple projects simultaneously?
A: No, VaultTrak tracks one project at a time for accuracy. However, you can quickly switch between projects, and the app will remember your last active timer for each project.

Data & Privacy

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Absolutely! VaultTrak uses:
  • End-to-end encryption for all data
  • Industry-standard security protocols
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure cloud storage with automatic backups
We never sell or share your data with third parties.
Q: Can I export my data?
A: Yes! You can export your data in multiple formats:
  • CSV (for Excel/Sheets)
  • PDF (professional reports)
  • JSON (developer-friendly)
Go to Reports → Export to download your data.
Q: What happens if I delete my account?
A: When you delete your account:
  • All your data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • You can export your data before deletion
  • Your subscription is canceled immediately
  • The action cannot be undone

Sync & Devices

Q: How does sync work?
A: VaultTrak syncs your data automatically across all your devices in real-time (with a paid subscription). Changes made on one device appear instantly on others. Free tier users can manually export/import data.
Q: Can I use VaultTrak offline?
A: Yes! VaultTrak works fully offline. Your time entries are stored locally and will sync automatically when you reconnect to the internet.
Q: How many devices can I use?
A: You can use VaultTrak on unlimited devices with a single account. All devices stay in sync automatically.

Reports & Invoicing

Q: What types of reports can I generate?
A: VaultTrak offers several report types:
  • Summary reports (totals by project/client)
  • Detailed time logs
  • Billable vs non-billable hours
  • Project budget tracking
  • Productivity analytics
Q: Can I create invoices in VaultTrak?
A: VaultTrak generates professional invoice-ready reports with:
  • Itemized time entries
  • Billing rates and totals
  • Client information
  • Custom branding (Business plan)
You can export these as PDFs or integrate with invoicing software.

Troubleshooting

Q: The timer isn't working. What should I do?
A: Try these steps:
  1. Check that VaultTrak has necessary permissions
  2. Restart the app
  3. Ensure you're on the latest version
  4. Check for system updates
If problems persist, contact support with your device details.
Q: My data isn't syncing. Help!
A: Common solutions:
  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify sync is enabled in Settings
  • Ensure you're logged into the same account on all devices
  • Check if your subscription is active (free tier doesn't include auto-sync)
  • Try manually syncing from Settings → Sync Now
Q: I lost some time entries. Can I recover them?
A: Yes! VaultTrak creates automatic backups. Go to Settings → Backup & Restore to restore from a previous backup. Backups are kept for 30 days (Pro) or 90 days (Business).
