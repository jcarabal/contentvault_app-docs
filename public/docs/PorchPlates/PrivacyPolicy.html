Privacy Policy for Porch Plates

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

Introduction

Porch Plates ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
  • Profile information (photos, bio, store description)

Content You Create:

  • Product listings (photos, descriptions, prices)
  • Meal plans and recipes
  • Family information (family name, member names)
  • Messages and comments in family chat and voting threads
  • Shopping lists

Transaction Information:

  • Order details
  • Payment information (processed securely through Apple's In-App Purchase system)
  • Subscription status

Information Collected Automatically

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address

Usage Information:

  • App features accessed
  • Time spent in the app
  • Error logs and crash reports

Location Information:

  • We do NOT collect precise location data
  • General location may be inferred from IP address for service delivery

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. Provide and Maintain Services:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process transactions and subscriptions
  • Enable communication between users (sellers and buyers)
  • Facilitate family meal planning and collaboration

2. Improve Our Services:

  • Analyze app usage and performance
  • Fix bugs and improve functionality
  • Develop new features

3. Communication:

  • Send order notifications and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important service announcements
  • Send push notifications (with your permission)

4. Security:

  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Protect user accounts
  • Ensure compliance with our terms of service

How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information with:

Within the App:

  • Sellers can see buyer names and order details
  • Buyers can see seller names, photos, and store information
  • Family members can see each other's names, votes, and messages within their family group

Service Providers:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud: We use Firebase for authentication, database, storage, and hosting
  • Apple: Payment processing for in-app purchases and subscriptions

Legal Requirements:

  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety

Data Storage and Security

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

Firebase Security:

  • All data is stored in Google Cloud (Firebase)
  • Encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Encrypted at rest on Firebase servers

Family Mode Encryption:

  • Child accounts use AES-256-GCM encryption
  • PINs are hashed using PBKDF2 with 100,000 iterations
  • Credentials stored in iOS Keychain

Authentication:

  • Firebase Authentication with secure token management
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text

Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • You can delete your account at any time
  • Deleted data is permanently removed from active databases within 30 days
  • Backup data may be retained for up to 90 days

Children's Privacy

Family Mode Child Accounts

Porch Plates Family Mode allows parents to create secure accounts for children under 13:

  • Parental Consent Required: Parents must create and manage child accounts
  • Limited Data Collection: Child accounts collect only essential information (name, PIN)
  • No Direct Marketing: We never send marketing communications to child accounts
  • Parental Controls: Parents can delete child accounts at any time
  • COPPA Compliance: We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Parents can contact us to review, modify, or delete their child's information.
